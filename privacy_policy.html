1. Types of Information We Collect


When you use our products and services, we may collect the following types of information.

4) Device and application information: When you use our device and application, we will obtain your device and application information, such as user configuration data, device MAC address, device version number, serial number, firmware version, language, APP version, mobile phone system version, device configuration, IP address, network type, device identification and other information.

Through this feature, we collect feedback questions you send us, and your contact information (if you provide them), which will be used to better understand your problems and contact you.


7) Log information: If you use our device or application, we will collect some usage data, such as creating or logging in to your account, account and device pairing, device restart times, network usage information, system errors, system Crashes, system restarts, system upgrades, etc. Your latest log information within 7 days will be automatically stored locally on your phone, and the old log information will be automatically deleted. We do not actively collect your log information.


8) Visitor information: When using the visitor function (applicable to unregistered or logged-in situations), visitors can experience our products and some limited services. Visitors' personal data (nickname, gender, height, weight, date of birth, sports health data, etc.) will be stored locally on your phone and will not be uploaded to our servers.


9) Application information: When using message notification, we will obtain the application list of your device, such as phone, contacts, other applications, etc., to obtain the application list, we may access the information notification status of the application.


Other Information: We may also collect other types of information that are not directly or indirectly linked to an individual, but are aggregated, anonymized or de-identified.
