Questions and help


1. How to connect to the application?

First open the APP, select "Device" at the bottom, then click Bind Device, enter the "Bind Device" page, select the watch Bluetooth in the list, and the connection can be successful.
Note: Before connecting, please check whether there is a paired watch in the Bluetooth of the mobile phone. If there is, please delete the pairing information and pair again.

2. How to solve the problem of frequent 

disconnection of Bluetooth connection?
The watch and Runmefit Pro App need to do the following settings to ensure the normal Bluetooth connection:
1) Please upgrade your watch and Runmefit Pro App to the latest version;
2) Please make sure that the Bluetooth switch of the watch and mobile phone is turned on;
3) Please turn on the GPS switch of the mobile phone, and open the Runmefit Pro App in the mobile phone settings to obtain the permission of the location;
4) Open the App to see if it can be automatically connected. If it can be automatically connected, it is usually because Runmefit Pro App fails to run normally in the background of the phone. Android phones need to add Runmefit Pro App to the protected background application. If the phone has other restrictions on Runmefit Pro App activities, please remove them at the same time.
If you cannot reconnect, please try to solve it according to the following methods:
a. Restart the Bluetooth of the mobile phone to reconnect.
b. Restart the watch to reconnect.
c. Restart the phone to reconnect.
d. Re-pair and connect after the App is unbound.
5) If none of the above methods can solve the problem, please reset the watch to factory settings and check whether there is a paired watch in the Bluetooth of the mobile phone. If there is, please delete the pairing information and pair again.

3. The watch cannot receive notifications

Please follow the steps below to check your phone settings:

1) Please check whether the watch and Runmefit Pro App are the latest version, if not, please upgrade to the latest version first.
2) Please check whether the Bluetooth connection between the watch and the mobile phone is normal, and whether the data can be synchronized normally through Runmefit Pro App.
3) Please check whether the message notification switch is turned on in the Runmefit Pro App message notification, and you need to select the application switch that needs to push messages in the message notification interface.
4) Please check whether the watch has turned on the do not disturb mode. When the Do Not Disturb mode is turned on, the message will not vibrate to remind you. You can check whether the Do Not Disturb mode is turned on on the device page.
5) The message push function can only push the information pushed to the status bar of the mobile phone to the wearable device. Please make sure that the message can be pushed to the status bar of the mobile phone.
Android users (take EMUI 9.0 as an example): Open the phone Settings> Notification Center to find the corresponding application, turn on the Allow notifications switch, and turn on the display of new message notifications on the status bar & banner & lock screen notification switch.
iOS users: Select phone settings> notifications, select the appropriate application, turn on the allow notification switch, and make sure that the display switch is turned on in the notification center.
6) Please add Runmefit Pro App to background protection.
Android users (take Huawei mobile phone as an example): Turn on the application auto-start and associated startup switch in Phone Manager> Permission Management, and turn off the Runmefit Pro App switch in Phone Settings> Battery> Lock Screen Cleanup Application; if you are in charge of the mobile phone Can’t find Runmefit Pro App, it means Runmefit App has been added to the background protection whitelist by default, no additional settings are required, but please confirm whether other housekeeper apps are installed on your phone. If there are other housekeeper apps, please add Runmefit Pro App to other housekeeper apps. Protected background applications.
7) Please allow system Bluetooth pairing when connecting. IOS13.0 and above will pop up a window whether to allow the device to display iPhone notifications. You need permission to use the call reminder and message reminder functions. If you miss this window, you can go to System Settings>Bluetooth>Select the corresponding Bluetooth device name>Share system notifications Turn on this feature.
8) If there is no problem with the above settings, please restart your phone and reconnect the device to try again.

4. There may be errors in watch step counting

1) The arm swing amplitude, frequency and regularity of the arm swing will have a certain impact on the step count. The accuracy of the step count of the bracelet depends on the person's walking posture, walking habits, walking speed, walking continuity, and road conditions. Are all related.
2) Please fill in all the information correctly and synchronize it to the bracelet, including gender, height, weight, and age, all of which will affect the calculation of the algorithm.
3) In our daily life, we often shake our arms or body. If in some scenes, the shaking is regular and the duration is relatively long, and the acceleration sensor data is similar to walking, the bracelet may mistake you for you Walking.
4) If you only take a few steps and the movement does not last while recording the steps, the bracelet may not record.
5) The watch does not have GPS positioning function, so there will be a certain error in step counting in sports mode.

5. Inaccurate heart rate detection

Non-standard wearing methods will affect the measurement of heart rate signals, such as light leakage caused by incomplete skin fit, movement of the bracelet on the skin, etc., wearing the bracelet too tightly, raising the arm and making a fist will affect blood circulation. All may affect the heart rate measurement. The correct measurement method is to lay the arm flat, relax and keep still, while ensuring that the bracelet is tightly attached to the wrist.

6. No heart rate and blood pressure data

Please check whether the heart rate timing detection function is turned on: Open Runmefit Pro App>Device>Turn on the heart rate timing detection. After it is turned on, the heart rate and blood pressure will be checked regularly.

7. Use camera control and music control functions

1) Open Runmefit Pro App>Device>Turn on music control/photograph control.
2) To use the camera control function, you need to obtain the permission to call the system camera. You can find the permission to open Runmefit Pro App in the phone settings.
3) The camera control function only supports normal control when the Runmefit Pro App is running in the foreground. If the Runmefit Pro App is running in the background, the bracelet cannot start and control the camera.

8. The phone does not respond when the watch looks for the phone

1) Please check whether the mute state of the mobile phone is turned off. In the mute state, if the mobile phone is unavailable, please turn off the mute state first.
2) Runmefit Pro App needs to obtain the permission to call the system sound (multimedia volume). You can find the permission to open the sound of Runmefit Pro App in the phone settings.

9. The time of the watch is not accurate

1) When the watch and Runmefit Pro App are successfully connected, the watch will synchronize the phone time.
2) After the bracelet is exhausted and turned off, the time will be incorrect if it is left for a long time. After charging, connect to the Runmefit Pro App to synchronize the phone time.

10. The weather information on the watch is not updated

If the weather information is not updated, please try the following check items:
1) Please make sure that Runmefit Pro App has allowed permission to obtain location information.
2) Please make sure that the mobile phone network connection is normal. Runmefit Pro App usually obtains weather data every 2 hours. If your network environment is not good, it may also cause the weather information data to be refreshed not in time.
3) Please make sure that the mobile phone has been allowed to obtain location information. The indoor closed environment may fail to locate the location due to GPS failure, so that the weather update cannot be obtained. Please go to an open area and try to reconnect.
4) If the update is not possible under the WIFI environment, please try to use the cellular network of the mobile phone.

11. The screen does not turn on when the watch is turned over

1) Please make sure to turn on the switch to turn on the wrist screen in the Runmefit Pro App device page.
2) Check whether the watch has the Do Not Disturb mode turned on. In the Do Not Disturb mode, the screen will not turn on when you turn your wrist.
3) Taking into account that you unconsciously turn your wrist during use, we have partially restricted the bright screen of the wrist. If you are walking or standing, it is recommended that you naturally raise your wrist from the side of your body to look at the screen (the screen should be as horizontal as possible) . If you are sitting, you can turn your wrists outward (preferably 90°) and then inwards (the screen should be up and as horizontal as possible). It is recommended that you try a few more times to master the best wrist turning angle.


12. Can't the app search the Bluetooth of the watch?
1) Make sure that the Bluetooth of your mobile phone system is turned on.
2) Make sure that the watch is not connected to the Bluetooth of the mobile phone system. If it is connected, please disconnect the Bluetooth of the system first, because the APP will automatically block the device connected to the Bluetooth of the system when searching.
